Characters

Seasons: 1
Jonathan Joestar

The noble and honorable Jonathan Joestar is the protagonist of "Phantom Blood." As the first JoJo, Jonathan's unwavering sense of justice and dedication set the foundation for the Joestar legacy. He battles his adoptive brother, Dio Brando, to protect his family and loved ones, showcasing immense courage and the power of Hamon.

2 3 4
Joseph Joestar

The cunning and quick-witted Joseph Joestar stars in "Battle Tendency." Known for his resourcefulness and humor, Joseph uses his sharp mind and Hamon abilities to fight against the ancient Pillar Men. His charisma and unconventional tactics make him a beloved and unforgettable character.

3 4 5 6
Jotaro Kujo

Jotaro Kujo, the stoic and powerful protagonist of "Stardust Crusaders," is known for his catchphrase "Yare Yare Daze" and his Stand, Star Platinum. Jotaro embarks on a worldwide journey to defeat Dio Brando once and for all, demonstrating immense strength, determination, and a deep sense of duty to his family.

4
Josuke Higashikata

The kind-hearted yet fierce Josuke Higashikata leads "Diamond is Unbreakable." With his Stand, Crazy Diamond, Josuke can heal and repair objects and people, making him a compassionate protector of his hometown, Morioh. His love for his friends and quirky personality shine throughout his story.

5
Giorno Giovanna

In "Golden Wind," Giorno Giovanna, the ambitious son of Dio, dreams of becoming a "Gang-Star" to reform the mafia from within. His Stand, Gold Experience, has life-giving abilities that reflect his desire to bring positive change. Giorno's determination and sense of justice drive him to challenge the corrupt forces in Italy.

6
Jolyne Cujoh

Jolyne Cujoh, the first female JoJo, stars in "Stone Ocean." As the daughter of Jotaro Kujo, she inherits his strength and resolve. Wrongfully imprisoned, Jolyne must clear her name and uncover a sinister plot within the prison walls. Her Stand, Stone Free, gives her the power to unravel her body into strings, showcasing her adaptability and tenacity.

7
Johnny Joestar

The protagonist of "Steel Ball Run," Johnny Joestar, is a former jockey seeking redemption and a new purpose in life. Paraplegic and determined, Johnny embarks on a cross-country race with his Stand, Tusk, which evolves through four powerful acts. His journey is one of self-discovery, healing, and relentless pursuit of his goals.

8
Josuke Higashikata (Part 8)

In "JoJolion," the second Josuke Higashikata is an amnesiac young man with a mysterious past. With his Stand, Soft & Wet, he can steal aspects of objects and living beings. Josuke's quest to uncover his identity and the secrets of Morioh Town is filled with intrigue, complexity, and personal growth.
